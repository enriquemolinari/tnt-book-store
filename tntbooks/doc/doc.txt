Hibernate:
- Typically, you shouldn't open and close sessions in your DAO classes because they don't know if other 
DAOs are going to need the same sessions. The best thing to do is open a session for each incoming request 
(using a filter is the easiest way to do this) and then calling sessionFactory.getCurrentSession() in all your 
DAOs to get the current session. That way all your DAOs will be easily reusable.

- Si quiero que los getters devuelvan collections inmutables cuando son persistidas, hibernate las trae de la BD. Lo cual es
logico porque voy a devolver una copia. Tambien hace unos Updates a la BD, no se bien por que...

- Si usamos las transactions manejadas con @Transactional de Spring, la property <prop key="hibernate.current_session_context_class">thread</prop>
hay que sacarla. http://jira.springsource.org/browse/SPR-4207
